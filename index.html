About us

Founded in 2021, Maridom Global Services Limited is a trusted provider of tires, lubricants, and fleet solutions across Nigeria. With over a decade of industry experience, we understand the unique challenges of Nigerian roads and fleet operations.

We serve transport, logistics, construction, and industrial sectors with premium tires, expert technical support, and reliable nationwide distribution. Our mission is simple: to keep fleets moving safely, efficiently, and cost-effectively
